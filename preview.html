Frequently asked

Honest answers to the questions every prospect asks.

What types of organisations does X24 Technologies typically work with? +
We partner with growth-stage startups scaling their engineering capacity, mid-market companies modernising legacy infrastructure, and enterprise teams building net-new AI or data products. Engagements typically begin at $20K and scale to multi-year retainers.
How does your engagement model work — fixed price or time & materials? +
Both. Clearly scoped projects are priced as fixed-scope engagements with milestone-based payments. Ongoing product development is structured as monthly retainers with a dedicated team pod.
Do you work with our existing engineering team or replace them? +
We augment, never replace. Most of our clients have internal engineers. We embed as a specialist extension — owning specific workstreams — while maintaining clean handoff documentation so your team retains full ownership.
How do you handle IP, source code ownership, and confidentiality? +
All IP, source code, models, and documentation produced during an engagement are fully transferred to the client upon completion. Mutual NDAs at project initiation; data handling aligned with GDPR and SOC 2 principles.
What does your post-deployment support look like? +
Every project includes a 30-day hypercare period at no cost. Beyond that, structured SLA-backed support retainers covering bug fixes, performance monitoring, model retraining, and feature iterations.
How long does a typical AI or software project take to deliver? +
AI proof-of-concept builds: 4–6 weeks. Production automation solutions: 6–10 weeks. Full-scale SaaS or data infrastructure: 3–6 months. Detailed roadmap provided during architecture phase.
